Design is one of my favorite topics of study. The process of creating a pleasing and intuitive user interface or a product label that stands out among the others is such a fascinating mix of art and psychology. I’ve been employed to work in many facets of design, including creation of marketing materials, logo design, product packaging, UI design and protoyping.

I recieved formal training through the UAA Graphic Design minor program, and expanded my skills working for UAA Student Activities, CRM Blackbox, and Stewart’s Photo Shop. I’ve also spent signficant time doing freelance design work for small businesses, artists, and musicians.
