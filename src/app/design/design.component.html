Aurelia Charters

This site was designed and built for a client out of Valdez, AK for his small charter business. The site allows the user to update content and add image galleries through an authenticated back end. The site was built over the course of about a month, keeping the clients budget in mind, and while I wasn't able to deliver all the features I wanted, I was able to complete everything that was promised.

Pure BHB Keto

This set of packaging designs were created for Pure BHB Keto while at Full Service Funnels. The product line was designed with results of split testing using similar products in mind, and implemented a sleek, modern look. Each product within the brand is color-coded to follow results of split testing, and provide maximum appeal to potential customers.
